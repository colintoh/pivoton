Pivoton

Pivoting your buttons since 2013

Oh My..

Default Options:

  • degree:30

    The degree of the pivot.

  • easing:'ease'

    The animation of the pivot. Supports any CSS3 transition.

  • speed:0.3

    Pivot speed in seconds

  • perspective:400

    The smaller the perspective, the crazier it gets.

  • activeClass:'active

    The class that will be added after clicking on the button.

  • debug:false

    Debugging the bug.

Events:

  • pivoton-up

    When pivoton button is pressed.

  • pivoton-down

    When pivoton button is release.
